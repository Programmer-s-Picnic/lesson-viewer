1

Tilak Ceremony

तिलक समारोह
Groom House Before Wedding Blessing
Formal acceptance and honor
English

The bride's family visits the groom's house, applies tilak to the groom's forehead, and offers gifts, sweets, and clothes as a ceremonial sign of respect and acceptance.

हिन्दी

वधू पक्ष के लोग वर के घर आते हैं, उसके मस्तक पर तिलक लगाते हैं, और सम्मान तथा स्वीकृति के रूप में उपहार, मिठाई और वस्त्र अर्पित करते हैं।

Forehead tilak Gifts and sweets Family respect

Purpose

To formally acknowledge the groom and honor him before the marriage proceeds further.

Who Performs It

Usually the bride's father or senior relatives, with blessings from both families.

Items Used

Kumkum, rice, sweets, coconut, clothes, garlands, and sometimes gifts.

Symbolism

Honor, welcome, and visible acceptance of the upcoming alliance.

2

Sagai / Ring Ceremony

सगाई / अंगूठी समारोह
Groom House Before Wedding Blessing
Public acknowledgment of the match
English

In many families, rings are exchanged and the marriage is publicly acknowledged among relatives. Sometimes this is held with the tilak event.

हिन्दी

अनेक परिवारों में अंगूठियों का आदान-प्रदान किया जाता है और विवाह की घोषणा रिश्तेदारों के बीच की जाती है। कभी-कभी यह तिलक के साथ होता है।

Engagement Relatives gather

Purpose

To formally mark the commitment between the couple and the two families.

Who Performs It

The bride and groom, with family members witnessing or facilitating the exchange.

Items Used

Rings, flowers, sweets, festive clothes, and gifts.

Symbolism

Commitment, mutual recognition, and joyful public acknowledgment.

3

Haldi Ceremony

हल्दी समारोह
Groom House Before Wedding Preparation
Purification and auspicious preparation
English

Turmeric paste is applied to the groom by close family members. The ritual is linked with festivity, blessing, cleansing, and preparation for the wedding day.

हिन्दी

परिवारजन वर को हल्दी लगाते हैं। यह रस्म शुद्धि, मंगल, आशीर्वाद और विवाह-दिवस की तैयारी से जुड़ी मानी जाती है।

Turmeric paste Festive songs Pre-wedding joy

Purpose

To purify and ceremonially prepare the groom before marriage.

Who Performs It

Parents, siblings, cousins, and close relatives.

Items Used

Turmeric paste, oil, flowers, ritual tray, and festive clothing.

Symbolism

Purity, joy, protection, and auspicious transformation into a groom.

4

Matkor / Sacred Soil Ritual

मटकोर / पवित्र मिट्टी की रस्म
Groom House Before Wedding Preparation
Connection to earth and family tradition
English

Women of the household bring sacred soil from a traditional spot. This regional custom connects the wedding preparations to the earth and to household blessings.

हिन्दी

परिवार की महिलाएँ किसी पारंपरिक स्थान से पवित्र मिट्टी लाती हैं। यह क्षेत्रीय रस्म विवाह-तैयारी को धरती और गृह-मंगल से जोड़ती है।

Women-led custom Local tradition

Purpose

To invoke fertility, grounding, and local family blessings.

Who Performs It

Usually women relatives of the household.

Items Used

Clay pot, soil, songs, ritual basket, leaves, and water.

Symbolism

The marriage is rooted not only in people, but also in place, land, and ancestry.

5

Mandap Sthapana

मंडप स्थापना
Groom House Before Wedding Sacred
Prepare the ritual center
English

The sacred wedding structure and puja items are arranged under priestly guidance, creating a ceremonial center for the marriage rites.

हिन्दी

विवाह के लिए पवित्र मंडप और पूजन सामग्री पुरोहित के निर्देशन में सजाई जाती है, जिससे संस्कारों का केंद्र तैयार होता है।

Puja items Ritual center

Purpose

To consecrate the physical space where sacred rites will unfold.

Who Performs It

The priest, assisted by family members.

Items Used

Mandap poles, cloth, kalash, flowers, lamps, rice, thread.

Symbolism

The home becomes a sacred site, temporarily transformed into ritual space.

6

Sehra Bandi

सेहरा बंदी
Groom House Before Wedding Preparation
Prepare the groom for departure
English

Before the baraat departs, the groom is dressed in ceremonial attire, the sehra is tied, and family members bless him with aarti and protective gestures.

हिन्दी

बारात प्रस्थान से पहले वर को विवाह-वेश में सजाया जाता है, सेहरा बाँधा जाता है, और परिवारजन आरती करके आशीर्वाद देते हैं।

Ceremonial attire Protective blessing

Purpose

To ceremonially transform the man into a groom ready to leave for marriage.

Who Performs It

Parents, sisters, and close relatives.

Items Used

Turban, sehra, ceremonial clothes, aarti plate, kajal in some families.

Symbolism

The groom enters a sacred social role, protected and blessed by his family.

7

Baraat Departure

बारात प्रस्थान
Groom House Wedding Day Transition
Journey to the bride's side
English

The groom departs with the wedding procession. Music, dance, decorated vehicles or a horse, and festive energy define the movement toward the bride's venue.

हिन्दी

वर बारात के साथ प्रस्थान करता है। संगीत, नृत्य, सजी हुई गाड़ी या घोड़ा, और उत्सवी वातावरण इस यात्रा को विशेष बनाते हैं।

Music and dance Festive procession

Purpose

To ceremonially carry the groom to the bride's place in public celebration.

Who Performs It

The groom, relatives, friends, musicians, and invited guests.

Items Used

Band, lights, horse or vehicle, flowers, fireworks in some families.

Symbolism

A public and joyful declaration that the marriage is now moving into its main phase.

8

Roka / Marriage Fixing

रोका / विवाह निश्चिती
Bride House Before Wedding Blessing
Initial family confirmation
English

This marks the initial family understanding and acceptance of the alliance, often accompanied by sweets and blessings.

हिन्दी

यह दोनों परिवारों के बीच प्रारम्भिक सहमति और संबंध की स्वीकृति का चरण होता है, जिसमें मिठाई और आशीर्वाद शामिल हो सकते हैं।

Initial agreementBlessings

Purpose

To mark the alliance as accepted and meaningful.

Who Performs It

Senior family members from both sides.

Items Used

Sweets, gifts, flowers, family blessings.

Symbolism

The relationship enters a socially recognized direction.

9

Mehendi Ceremony

मेहंदी समारोह
Bride House Before Wedding Preparation
Adornment, joy, and celebration
English

The bride's hands and feet are decorated with henna. This is one of the most joyful pre-wedding rituals and often includes songs, laughter, and dance.

हिन्दी

वधू के हाथों और पैरों पर मेहंदी लगाई जाती है। यह विवाह-पूर्व की सबसे आनंदमयी रस्मों में से एक है जिसमें गीत और नृत्य शामिल रहते हैं।

Henna designsWomen's celebration

Purpose

To beautify and celebrate the bride before the wedding.

Who Performs It

Female relatives, friends, and mehendi artists.

Items Used

Henna paste, cones, music, festive attire, flowers.

Symbolism

Joy, beauty, anticipation, and community celebration around the bride.

10

Haldi Ceremony

हल्दी समारोह
Bride House Before Wedding Preparation
Purity and auspicious bridal preparation
English

Turmeric is applied to the bride by family members. It symbolizes purity, blessing, and ceremonial preparation before the wedding day.

हिन्दी

परिवारजन वधू को हल्दी लगाते हैं। यह शुद्धि, मंगल और विवाह-दिवस की पवित्र तैयारी का प्रतीक है।

TurmericAuspiciousness

Purpose

To prepare the bride physically and ritually for marriage.

Who Performs It

Mother, sisters, cousins, and other relatives.

Items Used

Turmeric paste, oil, flowers, ritual plate.

Symbolism

Purification, auspicious glow, and sacred readiness.

11

Matkor

मटकोर
Bride House Before Wedding Preparation
Regional grounding and family identity
English

The bride's side also performs the sacred-soil custom, giving the preparations a strong regional and family-centered character.

हिन्दी

वधू पक्ष में भी पवित्र मिट्टी से जुड़ी यह परंपरा निभाई जाती है, जिससे विवाह-तैयारी में क्षेत्रीय और पारिवारिक पहचान जुड़ती है।

Regional customFamily tradition

Purpose

To connect the bride's marriage preparations to land, place, and tradition.

Who Performs It

Women of the bride's family and neighborhood in many traditions.

Items Used

Soil, baskets, pots, songs, leaves, ritual offerings.

Symbolism

The marriage is rooted in community, locality, and ancestral continuity.

12

Mandap Sthapana

मंडप स्थापना
Bride House Before Wedding Sacred
Prepare the wedding space
English

The mandap is arranged on the bride's side as the central place where the sacred marriage rituals will unfold.

हिन्दी

वधू पक्ष में मंडप की स्थापना की जाती है जो मुख्य वैवाहिक संस्कारों का केंद्र बनता है।

Sacred centerRitual layout

Purpose

To prepare the place where the marriage will be ritually completed.

Who Performs It

Priest and household members.

Items Used

Mandap, flowers, kalash, cloth, lamps, rice, ritual threads.

Symbolism

A household space is elevated into a sacred marriage arena.

13

Gauri Puja

गौरी पूजा
Bride House Before Wedding Sacred
Prayer for harmony in married life
English

The bride offers prayers for a peaceful and blessed married life. The ritual is devotional, inward, and full of hope for harmony.

हिन्दी

वधू सुखी और मंगलमय दाम्पत्य जीवन के लिए प्रार्थना करती है। यह श्रद्धा और वैवाहिक समरसता की कामना से जुड़ी रस्म है।

PrayerDevotional calm

Purpose

To seek blessing for a stable, loving, and auspicious married life.

Who Performs It

The bride, often guided by elders or priestly instruction.

Items Used

Flowers, lamp, offering plate, deity image or symbolic setup.

Symbolism

Inner readiness, devotion, and hope for lasting companionship.

14

Baraat Arrival and Welcome

बारात आगमन और स्वागत
Together Wedding Day Transition
Formal meeting of the two sides
English

The bride's family welcomes the groom and the baraat with ceremonial hospitality. This is a festive and formal point of meeting between the two sides.

हिन्दी

वधू पक्ष वर और बारात का स्वागत करता है। यह दोनों पक्षों के औपचारिक और उत्सवी मिलन का महत्त्वपूर्ण क्षण होता है।

AartiWelcome

Purpose

To receive the groom and honor the arrival of the procession.

Who Performs It

Usually the bride's mother and senior family members.

Items Used

Aarti plate, flowers, garlands, ceremonial welcome items.

Symbolism

The two families formally come face to face in ritual hospitality.

15

Jaimala / Varmala

जयमाला / वरमाला
Together Wedding Day Blessing
Mutual public acceptance
English

The bride and groom exchange floral garlands in one of the most recognizable and joyful moments of the wedding.

हिन्दी

वधू और वर एक-दूसरे को वरमाला पहनाते हैं। यह विवाह का सबसे प्रसिद्ध और आनंदमय क्षणों में से एक होता है।

Garland exchangeMutual acceptance

Purpose

To indicate mutual acceptance of one another in a visible, ceremonial way.

Who Performs It

The bride and groom themselves.

Items Used

Flower garlands, stage or mandap, music, family presence.

Symbolism

Reciprocal recognition and joyful public consent.

16

Kanyadaan

कन्यादान
Together Wedding Day Sacred
Sacred parental offering
English

The bride's parents formally offer the hand of the bride in marriage. It is widely regarded as one of the most sacred and emotional rites of the ceremony.

हिन्दी

वधू के माता-पिता कन्या का हाथ विवाह हेतु औपचारिक रूप से समर्पित करते हैं। इसे अत्यन्त पवित्र और भावनात्मक संस्कार माना जाता है।

Parental blessingSacred commitment

Purpose

To ritually entrust the bride into marriage with blessing and sanctity.

Who Performs It

Traditionally the bride's parents, guided by the priest.

Items Used

Hands joined with water, rice, flowers, cloth, mantra recitation.

Symbolism

Love, trust, parental responsibility, and sacred transition.

17

Vivah Havan

विवाह हवन
Together Wedding Day Sacred
Sacred fire as witness
English

The sacred fire is invoked as witness to the marriage. Mantras are recited and the central Vedic atmosphere of the wedding is established.

हिन्दी

पवित्र अग्नि को विवाह का साक्षी माना जाता है। मंत्रोच्चार के साथ विवाह का मुख्य वैदिक वातावरण निर्मित होता है।

AgniMantrasVedic core

Purpose

To perform the marriage under sacred witness and ritual sanctity.

Who Performs It

The priest directs; the bride and groom participate actively.

Items Used

Havan kund, firewood, ghee, grains, flowers, and mantras.

Symbolism

Agni represents truth, purity, witness, and divine presence.

18

Gathbandhan

गठबंधन
Together Wedding Day Sacred
Visible union of two lives
English

The garments of the bride and groom are tied together, symbolizing a shared life, shared duty, and sacred companionship.

हिन्दी

वधू और वर के वस्त्र आपस में बाँधे जाते हैं। यह संयुक्त जीवन, दायित्व और पवित्र साथ का प्रतीक है।

Knotted clothShared path

Purpose

To visibly show that the couple is now bound in one journey.

Who Performs It

Usually the priest or a senior married woman in the family.

Items Used

The bride's dupatta or sari end and the groom's stole or cloth.

Symbolism

Partnership, shared responsibilities, and inseparable ritual unity.

19

Phere

फेरे
Together Wedding Day Sacred
Marriage vows around sacred fire
English

The couple circles the sacred fire and takes the central vows of marriage. This is one of the most important and binding parts of the ceremony.

हिन्दी

दंपति पवित्र अग्नि के चारों ओर फेरे लेते हैं और विवाह के मुख्य व्रत ग्रहण करते हैं। यह विवाह का अत्यन्त महत्त्वपूर्ण संस्कार है।

VowsAgni as witnessMarriage bond

Purpose

To solemnize the marriage through sacred movement and vow-taking.

Who Performs It

The bride and groom, guided by the priest.

Items Used

Sacred fire, mantras, tied garments, grains, offerings.

Symbolism

The couple promises companionship, duty, sustenance, and lifelong partnership.

20

Sindoor and Mangalsutra

सिन्दूर और मंगलसूत्र
Together Wedding Day Sacred
Visible signs of married status
English

The groom applies sindoor and, where followed, ties the mangalsutra. These become visible sacred markers of marriage.

हिन्दी

वर वधू की माँग में सिन्दूर भरता है और जहाँ प्रचलन हो वहाँ मंगलसूत्र पहनाता है। ये वैवाहिक बंधन के प्रत्यक्ष पवित्र चिह्न माने जाते हैं।

Marriage symbolsVisible sanctity

Purpose

To mark the marital bond with visible ritual symbols.

Who Performs It

The groom, guided by customary or priestly direction.

Items Used

Sindoor, mangalsutra where practiced, ritual plate.

Symbolism

The marriage is now ritually sealed and publicly marked.

21

Vidai

विदाई
Post Wedding After Wedding Transition
Emotional departure from parental home
English

The bride leaves her parental home in one of the most emotional moments of the wedding, marked by blessings, tears, and gratitude.

हिन्दी

वधू अपने मायके से विदा होती है। यह विवाह के सबसे भावनात्मक क्षणों में से एक है जिसमें आशीर्वाद, अश्रु और कृतज्ञता के भाव जुड़े होते हैं।

FarewellFamily emotion

Purpose

To mark the bride's departure from one home to another.

Who Performs It

The bride, her parents, siblings, and the extended family.

Items Used

Rice, gifts, blessings, farewell gestures.

Symbolism

A deeply emotional family transition and a movement into a new household role.

22

Griha Pravesh

गृह प्रवेश
Post Wedding After Wedding Transition
Auspicious entry into the new household
English

The bride is welcomed into the groom's home through auspicious entry rituals associated with prosperity, blessing, and family acceptance.

हिन्दी

वधू का वर के घर मंगलमय रीति से स्वागत किया जाता है। यह समृद्धि, शुभागमन और नए घर में स्वीकृति का प्रतीक माना जाता है।

New householdAuspicious welcome

Purpose

To welcome the bride into the groom's family and home.

Who Performs It

Usually the groom's mother and female relatives.

Items Used

Aarti plate, rice pot, alta or symbolic entry arrangements.

Symbolism

The new household receives the bride as a bringer of prosperity and continuity.

23

Mooh Dikhai

मुंह दिखाई
Post Wedding After Wedding Blessing
Family introduction and welcome
English

Family members meet the new bride, offer blessings, and sometimes give gifts as part of her ceremonial welcome.

हिन्दी

परिवारजन नई वधू से मिलते हैं, उसे आशीर्वाद देते हैं और स्वागत-परंपरा के रूप में उपहार भी दे सकते हैं।

Family welcomeGifts

Purpose

To introduce the bride within her new family circle.

Who Performs It

Female relatives and household elders in many traditions.

Items Used

Gifts, sweets, veil arrangements, ceremonial greetings.

Symbolism

A new relationship network gently begins around the bride.

24

Reception

स्वागत समारोह / रिसेप्शन
Post Wedding After Wedding Blessing
Community celebration of the couple
English

A social celebration may follow the wedding so extended family, friends, and community members can greet and congratulate the couple.

हिन्दी

विवाह के बाद एक सामाजिक स्वागत समारोह आयोजित किया जा सकता है, जिसमें रिश्तेदार, मित्र और परिचित नवदंपति को शुभकामनाएँ देते हैं।

GuestsCelebration

Purpose

To celebrate the marriage with a wider social circle.

Who Performs It

The couple hosts or is presented to guests by family members.

Items Used

Stage, flowers, food, greetings, photography, music.

Symbolism

The marriage enters public social life beyond the immediate rituals.

Auspicious entry of the bride into the groom's home.

वधू का वर के घर मंगलमय प्रवेश।

Quick Wedding Quiz

Test the sequence and meaning of the rituals.

1. Which ritual is most directly associated with circling the sacred fire?

१. कौन-सी रस्म पवित्र अग्नि के चारों ओर घूमने से सबसे सीधे जुड़ी है?
Correct answer: Phere. This is the central vow-taking ritual around the sacred fire.

2. Which ritual usually marks the bride entering the groom's home?

२. कौन-सी रस्म वधू के वर के घर प्रवेश से जुड़ी होती है?
Correct answer: Griha Pravesh. It is the auspicious entry into the new household.

3. Which ritual is most associated with decorating the bride's hands with henna?

३. कौन-सी रस्म वधू के हाथों पर मेहंदी सजाने से जुड़ी है?
Correct answer: Mehendi Ceremony. It is a joyful pre-wedding adornment ritual.
Note: Different families in and around Varanasi may merge, reorder, rename, or expand rituals based on region, family tradition, sampradaya, and priestly guidance.
टिप्पणी: वाराणसी और उसके आसपास अलग-अलग परिवार अपनी परंपरा, क्षेत्रीय रीति, सम्प्रदाय और पुरोहित की सलाह के अनुसार कुछ रस्में जोड़, घटा, मिला या क्रम बदल सकते हैं।
