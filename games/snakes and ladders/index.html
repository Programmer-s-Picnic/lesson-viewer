Snakes and Ladders — Extra Moves Edition

Up to 4 players. Players can spend extra moves on snake escapes, ladder climbs, or a +1 move anytime during their own turn.

Exact roll needed for square 100. A player can win only on square 100 after all 10 extra moves are used.

Extra move rule
During your turn, you may spend 1 extra move anytime to move forward by exactly 1 square. If you land on a snake or ladder, you may also use an extra move there for the special action.

Rules

  • Standard board from 1 to 100.
  • Each player starts with 10 extra moves.
  • If you land on a snake head, you may spend 1 extra move to escape and stay there. Otherwise you slide down.
  • If you land on a ladder base, you may spend 1 extra move to climb it. Otherwise you stay where you landed.
  • During your own turn, you may also spend 1 extra move anytime to move forward by exactly 1 square.
  • You need an exact roll to land on 100.
  • You can reach square 100 before winning, but you cannot win until extra moves left becomes 0.
  • If fewer than 4 humans are selected, computer players fill the empty seats.
  • The Share button creates a link containing the current game state.
