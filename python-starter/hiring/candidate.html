How scoring works

Simple, transparent, and editable (JSON-based). You can tune the cutoffs per batch.

Profile: 20 Aptitude: 30 Coding: 30 HR: 20
  • Aptitude: timed MCQs + negative marking (configurable).
  • Coding: solve 2 tasks; pass testcases for points.
  • HR: chat answers scored by clarity + STAR keywords + length + confidence markers.
Tip: In Recruiter Mode, you can drag candidates across stages and generate ready-to-send messages.
